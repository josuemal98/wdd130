Roatan, Islas de la Bahia, Honduras

Roatán, part of Honduras' Bay Islands, is a Caribbean gem known for its stunning beaches, lush jungles, and world-class diving/snorkeling on the Mesoamerican Barrier Reef, offering activities like swimming with sharks and visiting sloth sanctuaries, with a unique culture blending English and Spanish, influenced by its British colonial history, and a thriving tourism sector focused on eco-tourism and relaxation, making it a top destination for nature lovers and adventureseekers.
